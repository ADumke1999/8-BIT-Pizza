The 8-BIT Pizza was founded in 2025. Its founder, Andrew Dumke, was born in 1999. The restaurant was themed after our founder's love for games. He started on the Nintendo Gamecube, but he later went back to play older games as far back as the Nintendo Entertainment System. Pizza was inspired by the times he and his friends would buy pizza while they played whatever games they felt like. Andrew wanted to capture these moments and be able to share them with the world. Although it was something so small and simple, he had made a lot of great memories and would like everyone else to experience the same things he did.
